Clean Edits

As the host of a hip-hop show that focuses primarily on more underground artists, I have to clean the majority of the music I play on my show myself, to ensure it complies with FCC standards. I have posted many of these edits online, and in some cases, artists' management teams have reached out to thank me and/or get copies of the clean versions of the files.
